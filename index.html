Other Scholarship Schemes

National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled. National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP).
Schemes on NPS: https://scholarships.gov.in/All-Scholarships Register & Login: https://scholarships.gov.in/otrapplication/#/

The Gujarat State Scholarship Portal on Digital Gujarat is an online platform that provides various scholarships to students from different categories, including SC, ST, OBC, and economically weaker sections. Managed by the Government of Gujarat, it aims to support students in pursuing higher education by offering financial assistance. The portal allows students to apply, track their applications, and receive scholarship benefits digitally.

To know more, visit: https://www.digitalgujarat.gov.in/

Buddy4Study Scholarship is a platform that connects students with various scholarships, including merit-based and need-based financial aid. It collaborates with government bodies, corporates, and NGOs to support students across different academic levels. The platform simplifies the application process, helping deserving students access financial assistance for their education.

For more information: https://www.buddy4study.com
